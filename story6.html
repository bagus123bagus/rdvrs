Thorn King's Heir

Chapter 1: The Whispering Wood

Raised in the edge-village of Thornmere, Corin believed he was just another orphan left behind by the war. But during his sixteenth winter, the trees began to whisper his name. One night, he was attacked by a beast cloaked in black vines, only to be saved by a traveling herbalist named Mira. She reveals the truth: Corin is the last living heir to the Thorn Throne, a kingdom consumed by darkness generations ago. The forest had kept him hidden, but now, the ancient curse is awakening.

Chapter 2: The Ruins of Aelwyth

Joined by Mira and a disgraced royal knight-turned-drunkard named Varek, Corin journeys to the fallen capital, Aelwyth. Along the way, they face corrupted fae, rebel encampments, and forgotten temples swallowed by the wild. In the ruins of Aelwyth, Corin sees the consequences of his father’s reign—citizens turned to husks by a cursed blood pact. In the heart of the temple, a sword called Sylthar, bound to the royal bloodline, calls out to him. But using it might awaken the dark power that sleeps within his blood.

Chapter 3: Crown of Thorns

Inside the palace, the spirit of the Thorn King—Corin’s father—appears, offering him a choice: take up the old crown and restore the kingdom’s power, or destroy the bloodline and free the land. Corin is tempted by visions of greatness and betrayed by one of his companions. As he teeters on the edge of corruption, Mira sacrifices herself to save him. With newfound clarity, Corin rejects the old path and reforges the crown from ash and light. Using Sylthar, he breaks the curse. The forest begins to heal, but Corin disappears, becoming a legend—the ghost prince who watches over the reborn kingdom from the shadows.
