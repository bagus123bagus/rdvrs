Shadows of the Glass Garden

Chapter 1: The Dreaming Vines

In the heart of Yoyo City, an abandoned greenhouse at the edge of the university is rumored to be haunted. When Akmal stumbles upon a glass key in a history museum, he, along with his best friends Beryl and Said, follow a map engraved in its handle to the garden. Inside, they discover plants blooming with colors never seen before—and vines that whisper thoughts. Soon, their dreams begin to grow into living things within the greenhouse, and the trio realizes the garden amplifies whatever the heart most desires.

Chapter 2: The Roots of Deception

As they explore deeper, Beryl dreams of her lost sister and finds a glowing orchid shaped like her face. Said, who wants to find his purpose, uncovers a vine that gives him glimpses of future inventions. Akmal dreams of peace—but the garden reveals a darker side. They find evidence that the greenhouse once belonged to a secret group of dreambinders—people who could manipulate reality through dreams. But their magic was too dangerous, and the garden was sealed. Until now.

Chapter 3: Bloom or Burn

The garden begins to grow out of control, manifesting nightmares and dangerous creatures. Akmal, Beryl, and Said are forced to fight their own inner fears to stop it. They find a hidden chamber where the dreambinders once met, and together, they seal the garden again—this time with their own memories. When they wake up, the greenhouse is gone, and their dreams are normal again. But sometimes, at night, Akmal still hears the vines whispering, asking if he wants to dream again.
